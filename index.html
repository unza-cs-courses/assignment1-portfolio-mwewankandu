About Me

My name is Mwewa Nkandu, and I come from a close‑knit family of five, being raised alongside my three brothers and one sister. After completing my secondary education at Mukuba Boys Secondary School, I pursued a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science at the University of Zambia, where I am currently majoring in Network Engineering.

My passion for web development grew from a desire to build a professional digital portfolio to market my technical skills and showcase my work. Beyond my personal projects, I actively provide web‑related services to clients, leveraging my academic background to deliver functional and impactful online solutions. I enjoy problem-solving and continuously learning new technologies – whether it's configuring network protocols or styling a responsive layout.

My goal is to combine my networking expertise with modern web development to create secure, efficient, and user‑friendly applications. I'm also a cybersecurity enthusiast, always exploring best practices to protect data and systems. In my free time, I mentor aspiring developers and contribute to open‑source projects. I believe that technology should be accessible, inclusive, and robust.
